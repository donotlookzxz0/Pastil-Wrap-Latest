About Us

Began in early 2024 as a personal entrepreneurship, Pastil Wrap started with the mission of bringing affordable Pastil flavors to the heart of Angeles, Pampanga. As the business continues its services, Pastil Wrap also aspires to expand by franchising to showcase our delicacy outside of Angeles, Pampanga as well as collaborating with other businesses. We pride ourselves on using the freshest ingredients and staying true to the traditional preparation of Pastil.

Pastil is a traditional Filipino dish from Mindanao, consisting of steamed rice topped with flavorful shredded chicken or beef, all wrapped in banana leaves. At Pastil Wrap, we take this classic dish and add our own modern twist, giving you a variety of flavors and options to choose from, all while staying affordable.
